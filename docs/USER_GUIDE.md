# Alyne User Guide

## Welcome to Alyne

Alyne is a wellness marketplace connecting you with qualified wellness professionals in your area. Whether you're looking for a personal trainer, yoga instructor, nutritionist, or other wellness services, Alyne makes it easy to find and book with the right provider.

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Download the App**
   - Available on iOS App Store and Google Play Store
   - Or use the web version at [alyne.com](https://alyne.com)

2. **Sign Up**
   - Open the app and tap "Sign Up"
   - Choose whether you're a **Client** (looking for services) or **Provider** (offering services)
   - Enter your email, password, and basic information
   - Verify your email address

3. **Complete Your Profile**
   - **Clients**: Add your wellness goals and preferences
   - **Providers**: Complete your professional profile, add services, credentials, and availability

## For Clients

### Finding Providers

1. **Discover Tab**
   - Browse available wellness providers in your area
   - Use the search bar to find specific services or specialties
   - Filter by:
     - Service type
     - Price range
     - Rating
     - Distance
     - Availability

2. **Provider Profiles**
   - View provider details:
     - Bio and specialties
     - Services and pricing
     - Credentials and certifications
     - Reviews and ratings
     - Availability

### Booking a Session

1. **Select a Provider**
   - Tap on a provider card to view their profile
   - Review their services, pricing, and reviews

2. **Choose a Service**
   - Select the service you want
   - View service details (duration, price, description)

3. **Pick Date & Time**
   - Choose from available time slots
   - Select your preferred date

4. **Add Notes (Optional)**
   - Add any special requests or notes for the provider

5. **Request Booking**
   - Tap "Request Booking"
   - Provider will receive notification and can accept or decline

### Managing Bookings

1. **View Your Bookings**
   - Go to the "Bookings" tab
   - See all your bookings (pending, confirmed, completed, cancelled)

2. **Booking Status**
   - **Pending**: Waiting for provider response
   - **Confirmed**: Provider accepted, session scheduled
   - **Completed**: Session finished, can leave review
   - **Cancelled**: Booking was cancelled

3. **Actions**
   - **Cancel**: Cancel a pending or confirmed booking
   - **View Details**: See booking information
   - **Message Provider**: Contact provider directly

### Messaging

1. **Access Messages**
   - Go to the "Messages" tab
   - See all your conversations

2. **Start a Conversation**
   - Tap on a provider from their profile
   - Or start from an existing booking

3. **Send Messages**
   - Type your message
   - Send photos or files (if supported)
   - See delivery and read status

### Payments

1. **Payment Process**
   - After provider confirms booking, you'll be prompted to pay
   - Payment includes:
     - Service fee (goes to provider)
     - Platform fee (Alyne's service charge)

2. **Payment Methods**
   - Credit/Debit card (Stripe)
   - Saved payment methods

3. **Payment Security**
   - All payments processed securely
   - Card information encrypted
   - No card data stored on our servers

### Reviews

1. **Leave a Review**
   - After a completed session, you can leave a review
   - Rate 1-5 stars
   - Add a written comment (optional)

2. **Edit Reviews**
   - You can edit your review later
   - Go to the booking and select "Edit Review"

## For Providers

### Setting Up Your Profile

1. **Complete Onboarding**
   - Add your bio
   - List your specialties
   - Add services with pricing
   - Upload credentials
   - Set your availability

2. **Profile Photo**
   - Upload a professional photo
   - Helps clients recognize you

3. **Service Area**
   - Set your service radius
   - Define where you're willing to travel

### Managing Services

1. **Add Services**
   - Go to Profile > Services
   - Add service name, description, price, and duration
   - Set service as active/inactive

2. **Update Pricing**
   - Edit service prices anytime
   - Changes apply to new bookings only

### Managing Bookings

1. **View Booking Requests**
   - Go to "Bookings" tab
   - See all booking requests

2. **Respond to Requests**
   - **Accept**: Confirm the booking
   - **Decline**: Reject the booking (with optional reason)

3. **Complete Sessions**
   - After session, mark booking as "Completed"
   - Client can then leave a review

### Bank Account Setup

1. **Link Bank Account**
   - Go to Profile > Payment Settings
   - Link your bank account securely via Plaid
   - Verify your account

2. **Receive Payments**
   - Payments are sent directly to your bank account
   - Instant transfers available
   - No manual withdrawal needed

### Managing Availability

1. **Set Recurring Availability**
   - Define your weekly schedule
   - Set available hours per day

2. **One-Time Availability**
   - Add specific dates/times
   - Block unavailable times

### Common Questions

**Q: How do I cancel a booking?**
A: Go to Bookings, select the booking, and tap "Cancel". Cancellation policies may apply.

**Q: When do I get charged?**
A: Payment is processed when the provider accepts your booking request.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. Contact support for assistance.

**Q: How do providers get paid?**
A: Providers receive payment directly to their linked bank account after session completion.

**Q: What if I need to reschedule?**
A: Contact the provider through the messaging feature to reschedule.
